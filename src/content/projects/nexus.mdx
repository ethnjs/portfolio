## the problem

PLACEHOLDER: Science Olympiad tournament directors manage dozens of volunteers across a full-day
competition — assigning them to events, tracking availability, and handling last-minute changes.
Before NEXUS, this was spreadsheets and manual emails. NEXUS centralizes the entire workflow.

  via Google Forms. Rather than rebuilding intake, NEXUS syncs directly from the sheet so
