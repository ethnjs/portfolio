## the problem

Running a Science Olympiad tournament means coordinating dozens of volunteers across events, 
time slots, and shifting availability. Most tournament directors still manage all of this through 
a patchwork of Google Forms, spreadsheets, and email chains — there's no dedicated tool for it.

<ProjectImage src="images/nexus-sync-pipeline.png" caption="image" />

NEXUS is the platform I'm building to replace that entire workflow, from volunteer intake 
through day-of logistics. It's designed to scale to hundreds of tournaments and thousands of 
users: tournament directors, event supervisors, and volunteers all in one place.

- **SQLite in dev, PostgreSQL in prod** — same SQLAlchemy models, zero code changes between
  environments. Keeps local iteration fast with no Docker dependency.

  via Google Forms. Rather than rebuilding intake, NEXUS syncs directly from the sheet so
  directors don't change their workflow.
